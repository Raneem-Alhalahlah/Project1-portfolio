    My projects


    The Smart Bus for Blind people


    -The smart bus project was pioneering and targeted the visually impaired group to help them move on public transportation without the need for anyone. The project was divided into a hardware part and a software part. The first part is a device that contains buttons that the blind person uses to press and determine his position in relation to the driver inside the bus, and the second part is an LCD screen inside the bus that shows the location of the blind person.
